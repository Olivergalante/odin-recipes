Pizza

The reason pizza is one of all americans top 10 foods is the love that is made with all pizzas around the world. Thin krispy pieces of pepperonies. Thich cheese and homemade tomatoe sause that tops it off perfectly.

Ingredients

  • Shreeded Cheese
  • Pepperonies
  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sause
  • Additional toppings
    • Bacon
    • Mushrooms
    • Sauage

How To Cook Steps:

  1. Grab some flour and add water till dough has solid form. Place pizza dough into fridge.
  2. Take frozen pizza dough (which should have been in the fridge for a few hours) allow dough to sit out and absorb some of the southern air and humidity.
  3. Dough will begain to soften agian, allowing you to form the dough to the pan you are using. While you are molding your pizza preheat oven to 350.
  4. Add toppings to your pizza. Sause, cheese, pepperonies. Once oven has heated up to 350 Degrees place pizza in oven for around 12-15 minutes.
